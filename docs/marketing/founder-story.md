# Founder Story — MyTools Group & MyTools Admin

## The Origin

The automotive service industry in France handles millions of appointments, quotes, and repairs every year — yet most of it is still managed with software built in the 2000s, paper ledgers, or personal spreadsheets.

MyTools Group was founded with a simple premise: the tools that keep France's cars on the road deserve better technology than the tools managing them.

---

## The Problem We Saw Up Close

Talking to garage owners across France, we heard the same frustrations again and again:

**"I have to drive back to the office to check if a client paid their invoice."**

**"I lose quotes because they're on a clipboard somewhere."**

**"My receptionist calls me three times a day for information that should be on my phone."**

The software solutions that existed were either too expensive for small garages, too complex for daily use, or desktop-only — completely useless when you're standing next to a car lift.

---

## Why Mobile First

We made the decision early: **mobile first, or nothing**.

Garage owners are not knowledge workers. They're on the floor, in the pit, talking to clients face-to-face. The only computer they consistently have with them is their smartphone.

If the software doesn't work on a phone — seamlessly, quickly, with no learning curve — it won't get used.

---

## Building MyTools Admin

We designed MyTools Admin with one principle: **every feature must be accessible in under 30 seconds**.

Creating a quote. Marking an invoice paid. Confirming a reservation. Finding a client's phone number. Every workflow was pressure-tested against the question: *can a garage owner do this while a customer is waiting?*

The dark interface wasn't just an aesthetic choice — it's practical. Garages are often poorly lit. A bright white screen at 7am is painful. A clean dark interface with high-contrast red accents is readable in any lighting condition.

---

## The Security Philosophy

We took an unusual stance: **no public sign-ups**.

MyTools Admin is not a consumer app. It manages real financial data — invoices, client PII, revenue figures — for real businesses. We believe that access to this kind of data should be controlled and verified, not open.

Every MyTools Admin user is manually provisioned by our team and associated with a verified partner garage. This means slower growth, but also zero risk of unauthorized access to sensitive business data.

This isn't a limitation — it's a feature.

---

## What's Next

MyTools Admin 1.0 covers the essentials. Our roadmap includes:

- Push notifications for new bookings and quote approvals
- PDF generation for invoices and quotes directly from the app
- Multi-garage management for group operators
- Expanded analytics with service performance breakdowns
- Offline mode for areas with poor connectivity

We're building this with and for our garage partners. Every feature on the roadmap came from a real conversation with a real garage owner.

---

## For the Automotive Industry

This is a niche product for a niche market — and that's exactly the point.

The automotive service industry is massive, underserved by software, and increasingly competitive. Independent garages need professional tools to compete. MyTools Admin is our contribution to leveling the playing field.

**Built for performance. For the people who keep France moving.**
