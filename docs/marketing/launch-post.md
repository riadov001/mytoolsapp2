## Launch Post (First Comment)

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm thrilled to introduce **MyTools Admin** — the mobile app we built for garage professionals who are tired of managing their business from a desktop they never open.

### The problem we kept hearing

Garage owners told us the same thing over and over:

> "I check invoices on my laptop at 11pm. I miss quotes while I'm under a car. My receptionist calls me to confirm appointments I could approve from my phone in 2 seconds."

The tools existed — but they were designed for accountants, not mechanics.

### What we built

A mobile-first dashboard that connects to the MyTools Group back-office in real time:

📊 **Dashboard** — Revenue KPIs, 6-month chart, invoice and quote status at a glance

📄 **Quotes** — Create, edit, attach photos, change status. Done in under a minute.

🧾 **Invoices** — Track paid, pending, and overdue. No more spreadsheets.

📅 **Reservations** — Full appointment management with an integrated calendar picker.

👥 **Clients** — Complete client database. Search, create, edit from your phone.

### The design philosophy

We took inspiration from iOS's Settings app — clean, grouped lists, zero visual noise. We added a dark theme because garages are... not always the brightest environments.

Every interaction is designed to work with one hand, while the other is holding a wrench.

### Who can use it

Right now, MyTools Admin is available exclusively to administrators and employees of MyTools Group partner garages. This is intentional — we're building trust before we scale.

We'd love your feedback on the product direction, the UX, and what you'd want to see next.

Happy to answer any questions below! 🔧
