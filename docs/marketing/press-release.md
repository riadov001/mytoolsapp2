## MyTools Group Launches MyTools Admin: The Mobile App Putting Garage Management in Professionals' Pockets

*A new B2B mobile application gives French garage owners real-time access to their business data — quotes, invoices, clients and appointments — from any iOS or Android device*

---

**[France] — March 2026** — **MyTools Group**, a leading automotive services platform in France, today announced the launch of **MyTools Admin**, a mobile application that gives garage administrators complete control over their business operations from their smartphone.

### Designed for the Real World of Garage Management

For too long, garage professionals have been forced to manage their business from desktop software designed with accountants in mind — not mechanics. MyTools Admin changes that by delivering a fast, mobile-first interface that puts critical business data in the hands of the people who need it most.

"A garage owner shouldn't have to go back to their office to check if an invoice was paid or to create a quote," said the MyTools Group team. "We built MyTools Admin for the person standing in the garage bay, phone in hand, who needs real information in real time."

### Complete Business Management on Mobile

MyTools Admin provides authorized garage administrators with:

- **Analytics Dashboard** — Real-time revenue KPIs including monthly revenue, pending revenue, and a 6-month trend chart
- **Quote Management** — Create, edit, and track customer quotes with photo attachments and status tracking
- **Invoice Tracking** — Monitor paid, pending, and overdue invoices with one-tap status updates
- **Appointment Scheduling** — Full reservation management with an integrated calendar picker
- **Client Database** — Complete customer records accessible and editable from the field

### Enterprise Security for Sensitive Business Data

MyTools Admin takes a closed-access approach to security. Unlike consumer apps, MyTools Admin accounts are provisioned exclusively by the MyTools Group customer service team — ensuring that only verified professionals access sensitive business data.

The app uses JWT authentication with automatic token refresh, encrypted session management, and communicates exclusively with MyTools Group's secure European servers.

### Availability

MyTools Admin is available now on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, exclusively for administrators and employees of MyTools Group partner garages in France.

To request access for your garage, contact the MyTools Group customer service team at contact@mytoolsgroup.eu.
